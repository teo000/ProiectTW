Welcome to our Help Page!

Here you can find the answer to frequently asked questions.

How to Sign Up?

Signing up is simple! All you need to do is go to our Login Page, click on "Sign up now!" and you will be redirected to our signing up page. You will need to choose a username, a password and a few genres which you enjoy reading.

How do I rate a book?

Each book has a specific page dedicated to it.On that page you can choose whether you are currently reading that book, you want to read it or, if you've already read it, you can give it a rating. You can change your rating any time!

How do create a reading group?

  1. Go to "My Groups".
  2. Choose a group name.
  3. Click on "Create Group".
