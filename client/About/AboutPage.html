Welcome to our About Page!

Here you can find more information about our project.

Why did we choose this project?

Because we are passionate about reading and social media apps and we think that this project combines both areas.

What does the website do?

The website is an online community for book enthusiasts. Users can join reading groups to connect with other like-minded individuals and engage in discussions about their favorite books. They can write reviews and rate books to help others discover new reads, and easily search the vast collection of books by title, author, genre, or keyword. In addition, the website generates various statistics such as the most popular books or authors and provides regular updates which are available through an RSS feed. Whether one is a casual reader or a dedicated bookworm, the website provides the perfect platform to explore, connect, and share their love for literature with others.
